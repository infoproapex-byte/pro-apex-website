The fine print, made simple

Clear terms, before anything starts

📝 Written agreement required

Labour only: I bring tools, skill and labour. Materials are supplied by you or delivered directly to the property — I don't purchase or transport materials myself.

Contract first: Before work starts, we sign a simple written agreement listing exactly what will be done, the finish standard, colours, details, and the price — agreed together, in advance.

Payment terms: Once the work matches what's in the contract, payment is due in full immediately. Unpaid amounts outside agreed terms will be pursued through the small claims process.

✅ My guarantee

I complete exactly what's agreed. If anything doesn't match the contract, I return and put it right free of charge within 12 months of completion.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you supply materials?

No — this is a labour-only service. You supply materials, or have them delivered directly to the property, and I provide the tools and skilled labour to fit them.

How quickly can you visit for a quote?

I usually aim to visit within 48 hours of your call to look at the job and agree details in writing.

Are you insured and qualified?

Yes — Pro APEX is a fully insured Ltd company, and all electrical work is carried out to 18th Edition wiring regulations.

Do you do gas or air conditioning work?

No, these fall outside my qualifications. I can recommend trusted Gas Safe and F-Gas registered specialists if you need either.

What if I'm not happy with the finished work?

All work is guaranteed for 12 months. If something doesn't match what we agreed in writing, I'll come back and fix it at no extra cost.
